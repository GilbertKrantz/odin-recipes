Scrambled Eggs

    Description

    The best Scrambled Eggs recipes that has been perfected for hundred of years.

    Ingredients

    • 2 Eggs
    • 2 Tablespoons Sliced Cheese
    • 2 Thin Slices Ham
    • 1 Teaspoon Heavy Whipping Chream
    • 1/2 Teaspoon Butter

    Steps

    1. Whisk eggs together in a small bowl until smooth. Mix in Cheddar cheese, ham, and heavy cream.
    2. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; cook and stir until set but still moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
